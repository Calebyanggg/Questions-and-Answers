1. 为什么你想去美国学习？Why do you want to study in the US?

I would like to study in the US because the U.S. is better in university education, has high-level professional courses, and a lot of general education courses. The choices are also very flexible.

I have considered other countries, but university education really is better in the US. It has the latest resources and lots of practical opportunities. I believe studying in the U.S. will help me grow in a way I can’t anywhere else.

I had always been preparing for GaoKao, wanting to study in a Chinese University, but I became sick right before it due to physical and mental pressure, so I was unable to take it. Only in September did I make up my mind to study in the US, so I applied to a school and now plan to enroll in the spring semester.
5. 💥为什么选择社区大学？Why do you choose Community College?
在我的案例里，社区大学是去更好大学的跳板。我以前是班里的尖子生，但错过了高考，崩溃后在家休息了。九月份，我决定不复读了，想出国读书，但时间太紧，没申请到理想的学校。社区大学入学要求低，容易达到，专业多，教学水平不错，还可以转学分，学费也实惠，我打算两年后转学到四年制大学。
Community colleges are a springboard to better universities in my case. I used to be the top student in my class, but I burned out before Gaokao and missed it, so I took a break at home. In September, I decided not to reset and wanted to study abroad, but the time was too tight to apply to a dream school. This community college offers many majors, good standards and reasonable tuition fees, so I chose it, and will transfer to a four-year university in 2 years.
shorter one:
Community colleges are a springboard to better universities in my case. It is easy to get into, offers many majors, good standards, offers credit transfers, and the tuition fees are reasonable. I plan to transfer to a four-year university in two years.
6. 💥为什么选择这所社区大学呢？Why did you choose this school?
CSCC是新英格兰地区最大的社区大学之一，新英格兰地区是美国大学教育的发源地，教育水平高，好大学多。学校每年都有学生成功转学到耶鲁、康奈尔、康大等名校，成功率还不错，当地口碑也很好。再加上学费合理，对我来说是个不错的机会。
CSCC is one of the largest community colleges in the New England area, which is the birthplace of American higher education. And it has many top universities. Each year, a number of its students successfully transfer to schools like Yale, Cornell, and UConn with a high success rate, and CSCC has a good local reputation. And the tuition is affordable,so it is a great opportunity for me.
